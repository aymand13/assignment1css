About the Song

"Imagine" is a song written and performed by John Lennon. It was released in 1971 as the lead single from his album of the same name. The song envisions a world of peace and unity without the barriers of religion, nationality, or possessions.

About the Artist

John Lennon was a member of the legendary band The Beatles. He was an influential singer-songwriter, peace activist, and a key figure in the counterculture movement of the 1960s.
